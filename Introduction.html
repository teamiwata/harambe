Twenty years after the death of Harambe, Donald Trump created an island zoo. This island zoo is called the New Cincinnati Zoo. This zoo is devided up into four different islands composed of different animal exhibits ranging from mammals, reptiles, aquatic species and insects. On the grand opening of the New Cincinnati Zoo, the Ghost of Harambe rises from the dead in the old abandoned Cincinnati Zoo. He sees the New Cincinnati Zoo from the distance and decides to set the animals free. In order to set them free he posseses one of the gorillas in the gorilla exhibit to have a physical form.
